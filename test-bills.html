How to View the Bills Sub-Page

The Bills sub-page is part of the Customer Details page. Follow these steps to access it:

1 Go to http://localhost:3000
2 In the sidebar on the left, click on Customers
3 In the customers list, click the View (Eye icon) button on any customer row
4 Once on the Customer Details page, you'll see four tabs:
  • Customer Profile
  • Rates
  • Payments
  • Bills (NEW!)
5 Click on the Bills button to view the Bills sub-page with the duplicated table

What's on the Bills Page:

  • Yellow-themed Customer Bills header card with statistics
  • Recent Bills table with all the same functionality as Payments:
    • Search across all columns
    • Sort by any column (except Actions)
    • Export to Copy, Print, Excel, CSV, PDF
    • Shows 8 sample bill records
    • Pagination controls
    • Sort indicator in footer
